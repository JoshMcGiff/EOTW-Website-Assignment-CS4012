About Us


Our Founders Pictured Above On their daily save the planet walk
This Project was founded on Ocotober 28th 2019 when we were heavily inspired by our Modelling Teacher to create an organisation that will actually make a change to this planet and finally save it from climate change. We promote an eco-friendly lifestyle by running annual events that fund our various projects that help to sustain our planet. We feel our mission is one everyone should be a part of and we hope you cna join us on our journey to make the world a better place.

Ⓒ 2019 End Of The World

End Of The World is a non-profit organisation that strives to protect our planet from climate change. We promote an eco-friendly lifestyle by running annual events that fund our various projects that help to sustain our planet.
